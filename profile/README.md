## 🎓 Who we are

**Networkers Home** is a specialist training organization for networking and cybersecurity professionals. We run instructor-led and self-paced programs covering:

- Cisco networking (CCNA, CCNP Enterprise, CCNP Security, CCIE tracks)
- Firewall certifications (Palo Alto PCNSA/PCNSE, Fortinet NSE, Check Point CCSA/CCSE)
- SASE and Zero Trust (Zscaler ZDTA, Cloudflare Zero Trust, Netskope)
- Cloud networking (AWS Advanced Networking, Azure, GCP)
- SD-WAN (Cisco Viptela, Versa, Fortinet SD-WAN)
- Cybersecurity (Security+, CEH, CISSP, SOC analyst tracks)
- DevOps & infrastructure (Kubernetes, Terraform, Ansible)

Visit **[www.networkershome.com](https://www.networkershome.com)** for the full course catalog.

## 🌍 Why free?

Hands-on practice is the single biggest differentiator between someone who passes a certification and someone who can actually do the job. We believe that practice should not be paywalled.

So we open the simulators to everyone — students, career switchers, working professionals, even competitor training providers. If these tools help you grow, we invite you to check out our [training programs](https://www.networkershome.com/courses) — that's how we sustain the free work.
