## 💡 Why we build free tools

> *Hands-on practice is the single biggest differentiator between someone who passes a certification and someone who can actually do the job.*

Most lab environments require expensive hardware, per-seat vendor licenses, or complex local setups. Students at 11 pm the night before an exam don't have time for that.

So we build **browser-based simulators** that launch in one click and let you practice the actual skills being tested. We keep them **free and open** — for students, career switchers, instructors, and even competing training providers.

**If these tools help you grow**, consider exploring our [training programs](https://www.networkershome.com/courses) — that's how we keep the lights on and the simulators running.
